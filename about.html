What is Splatoon?

The Splatoon series is a multiplayer, third-person shooter that also has singleplayer modes. In the Splatoon series, you play as humanoid cephalopods, primarily as inklings, which are squids, and octolings, which are octopuses. In the case of this boss guide, we talk about the boss fights in one of the singleplayer modes of Splatoon 2.
