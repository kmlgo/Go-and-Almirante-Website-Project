Octo Expansion

In this Story DLC, you begin as an Octoling without their memories, in a metro deep under the sea. You meet an eccentric old man by the name of Captain Cuttlefish, and a strange phone claiming to be your guide to the "Promised Land" and calling you the 10,008th applicant. You, now called Agent 8, encounter and team up with idols Pearl and Marina from Off the Hook on your quest through the Deep Sea Metro to uncover your memories and, perhaps, reach that "Promised Land" that the phone spoke of...
